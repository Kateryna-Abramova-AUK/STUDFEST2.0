Student IT Fest 2025

Student-organised IT festival for students of Ukraine. Join us and let's create something amazing together!

27 October - 8 November 2025

Kyiv, Ukraine

About Us

We are Olympiad Programming Club at AUK and we are passionate about creating and improving. We wanted to create an opportunity for students to learn and grow together.

🚀

Practice & Compete

Take part in educational lectures, hackathons, and coding challenges to test your skills and compete with others.

🤝

Innovative Community

Explore fresh ideas, share your vision, and collaborate with like-minded individuals.

🎯

Goal-Oriented Focus

Work towards your goals with clear objectives and a supportive community.
